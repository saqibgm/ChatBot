Privacy Notice

Last updated: December 2024

This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our GLPI Support Chatbot.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us during your chat sessions, including:

  • Chat Content: The queries, messages, and files you send to the chatbot.
  • Session Data: Information about your interaction, such as timestamps and duration.
  • User Identifiers: Your user ID or session ID to maintain conversation context.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve user support services.
  • To process and respond to your inquiries and support tickets.
  • To analyze chatbot performance and improve response accuracy.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our systems.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., LLM providers, hosting services) who assist in operating specific functions of the chatbot.
  • Internal Departments: Relevant support teams to resolve your tickets.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain chat logs for a limited period to ensure service quality and for audit purposes, after which they are anonymized or deleted.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice, please contact the IT Support team.
