Cookies Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when you use our GLPI Support Chatbot.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer or mobile) when you visit a website or use an application. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the chatbot to function correctly, such as maintaining your session and conversation history.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences (e.g., language settings, theme) to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use these to understand how users interact with the chatbot to improve its performance.

3. Your Choices

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the chatbot's features, such as conversation history.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
